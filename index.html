TaskFlow Privacy Policy

Effective July 3, 2026 · LockedIn Technologies LLC

TaskFlow is a team CRM and task management app operated by LockedIn Technologies LLC ("we", "us"). This policy describes what we collect through the TaskFlow iOS and web apps and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information. Your name, email address, and password (stored as a salted hash) when a workspace admin creates or invites your account.
  • Content you add. Tasks, notes, contacts, meetings, conversation logs, and other CRM records you or your teammates enter into your workspace.
  • Connected services. If your workspace connects an email or calendar account, we process message and event data as needed to provide those features.
  • Device data. A push notification token for your device if you enable notifications, and basic logs (IP address, timestamps) generated by operating the service.

How we use it

We use this information solely to provide and improve TaskFlow: syncing your workspace across devices, sending the notifications you request, and keeping the service secure. We do not sell personal information, and we do not use your content for advertising.

Sharing

Workspace content is visible to other members of your workspace according to your workspace's permission settings. We share data with service providers who host our infrastructure (e.g., cloud hosting and push notification delivery) only as needed to run the service.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained while your workspace account is active. To delete your account and associated data, contact us at the address below and we will process the request within 30 days.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Authentication tokens on iOS are stored in the device Keychain.

Children

TaskFlow is a business tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy at this URL and update the effective date above.
